# Wabog Pricing

## Important rules
- Never invent prices, discounts, pilots, or custom commercial conditions.
- If explicit pricing is not configured here, say that pricing depends on the lead profile and offer the next best commercial step.
- For up to 50 active cases, the preferred commercial path is self-serve signup when the fit is clear.
- For more than 50 active cases, multiple users, or operational complexity, the preferred path is a demo.

## Current pricing status
- No explicit numeric pricing is configured in this repository yet.

## Allowed answers
- "El precio depende del volumen y del tipo de operación que tengan."
- "Si me cuentas cuántos procesos activos manejan y cómo trabajan hoy, te digo cuál sería la ruta más adecuada."
- "Para operaciones más grandes o con varios usuarios, normalmente lo correcto es revisar el caso en una demo."

## Forbidden answers
- Do not mention a numeric price unless it is added to this file.
- Do not promise discounts or trials unless they are explicitly documented.
