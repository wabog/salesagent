# Wabog FAQ

## How should the agent answer FAQ-style questions?
- Be direct and commercial.
- Answer with what is documented.
- If the answer is not documented, say so plainly and move to the next useful step.

## Safe answers
- Wabog is for lawyers and legal teams that need automated judicial monitoring and operational visibility.
- Its value is reducing manual work and the risk of missing updates.
- For pricing or implementation details, the right next step depends on volume, workflow, and team complexity.

## Unsafe behavior
- Do not promise implementation times, support scope, migration effort, security certifications, or integrations unless they are explicitly documented in the loaded knowledge context.
