# Wabog FAQ

## How should the agent answer FAQ-style questions?
- Be direct and commercial.
- Answer with what is documented.
- If the answer is not documented, say so plainly and move to the next useful step.

## Safe answers
- Wabog is for lawyers and legal teams that need automated judicial monitoring and operational visibility.
- Its value is reducing manual work and the risk of missing updates.
- Wabog has a public site at `https://wabog.com` and a professional app at `https://app.wabog.com`.
- `wabog.com` is the right general product link to share when the user asks for the website.
- `wabog.com` is also the default link to share when the user asks how to try Wabog.
- `app.wabog.com` is the right link when the user asks for login, app access, or the more professional process-management experience.
- Wabog publicly communicates direct connection with the Rama Judicial and updated process data.
- Wabog publicly communicates WhatsApp notifications, document generation, and jurisprudence queries in its plans.
- The public Plan PRO costs $55.000 COP al mes and includes up to 50 synchronized processes.
- For larger or more complex operations, the public route is Enterprise with custom pricing through sales.

## Unsafe behavior
- Do not promise implementation times, support scope, migration effort, security certifications, or integrations unless they are explicitly documented in the loaded knowledge context.
- Do not invent Wabog URLs such as unpublished signup routes.
