# Wabog Company

## What Wabog does
- Wabog is an AI-powered platform for lawyers and law firms.
- Wabog today has two connected faces:
  - `https://wabog.com`: the public Wabog experience, centered on judicial monitoring, WhatsApp interaction, AI-assisted follow-up, and lead capture around process monitoring. This is the default first link to share when someone wants to try Wabog.
  - `https://app.wabog.com`: the professional app for judicial process management, organization, memorials, tasks, and operational follow-up.
- It automates vigilance and monitoring of judicial processes in real time.
- It helps users avoid missing judicial deadlines and updates.
- It lets the user sync a radicado and receive updated information from the judicial system.
- The main public value proposition is to stop manually checking court systems and get notified when something changes.

## Who it is for
- Independent lawyers.
- Small law firms.
- Law firms with higher process volume or operational complexity.
- Legal teams that need better visibility and less manual follow-up.

## Pain points it solves
- Time wasted checking court systems manually.
- Risk of missing judicial updates.
- Fragmented processes across WhatsApp, Excel, email, and legacy tools.
- Low visibility across teams and caseload.
