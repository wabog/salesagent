# Wabog Company

## What Wabog does
- Wabog is an AI-powered platform for lawyers and law firms.
- It automates judicial case monitoring, deadline tracking, real-time court updates, and internal case organization.
- The main value proposition is to stop manually checking court systems and get notified when something changes.

## Who it is for
- Independent lawyers.
- Small law firms.
- Mid-size and large firms with operational complexity.
- Legal teams that need centralized case visibility.

## Pain points it solves
- Time wasted checking court systems manually.
- Risk of missing judicial updates.
- Fragmented processes across WhatsApp, Excel, email, and legacy tools.
- Low visibility across teams and caseload.

## Positioning guidance
- Lead with automation, speed, and risk reduction.
- Tie the value to current workflow and case volume.
- Do not oversell advanced capabilities that are not explicitly documented in this knowledge base.
